Biography

I am a PhD student at Inria working in the Magnet and Multispeech team. I am supervised by Dr. Aurélien Bellet, Dr. Emmanuel Vincent and Dr. Marc Tommasi. My work mainly focuses towards privacy-preserving speech processing.

Check out the 1st Voice Privacy Challenge to know more about the privacy benchmarks.

To know more about my previous works, please visit this page.
